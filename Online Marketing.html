Online Marketing Tools

=============================================================================================================================================================================================================

Website

The Companywebsites are a central section of the online marketing. The company website is used to advertise new customers both for customer loyalty. It is suitable for the business - and private target groups.

Advertising on Search Engines (PPC)

PPC (pay per click) campaign is a form of advertising that includes the presentation of ads on the first search engine pages for potential buyers who are interested in a specific product or service, and as the name suggests, the ads are only charged per performance, ie per click. This form of advertising can be found on social networks, portals and so on .... I think that this form of online marketing is mostly suitable for the private target groups.

Marketing Content

Content marketing or marketing content implies creating original,relevant, and useful content that will easily attract customers' attention and present them in an unobtrusive way to specific products or services. For example: creating e-books, blogging, online and offline journals are a form of this form of marketing. it is suitable for the business - and private target groups.

Marketing of social networks

Because of the explosive growth of popularity and use of social networks over the past few years, they are today one of the most powerful tools for promoting a company and improving its business on the Internet. Creating profiles on a wide variety of social networks, such as Facebook, Twitter, and other community management. Product and service promotion or improving the position of the site itself, is at the heart of this type of marketing. It is mostly suitable for the private target groups.

SEO - Website Optimization for Website Search Engines

Website Optimization (SEO) is a continuous process of improving the site's position on web search engines to make it the highest place in search results and resulting in increased visibility and visibility. Website optimization includes keyword research, competitor analysis, target user definition, optimization of internal links and landing pages I think that it is also suitable for the business - and private target groups.

Email marketing

Email Marketing is a dynamic marketing platform that includes sending messages to the email address of a user who has pre-booked to the list of recipients. E-mail marketing is a useful channel for product and service promotion and awareness-raising for a particular brand. It is good to show a brand or a product to the customer better. I think that it is also suitable for the business - and private target groups.

Poster

A poster or poster is a piece of paper that is glued to a wall, column or tablet, and can serve different purposes and be of different sizes. The term "poster" is usually used to describe paper pieces that serve for promotional purposes, such as those that display a product or announce an upcoming public event. It is suitable for the business - and private target groups.

Flyer advertising

Flyer to spread out at different places. So it is possible for example to make your company known to a relatively large amount of people without too high a cost. It is also suitable for the business - and private target groups.
